Sinclair - Volkswagen Newport

The names Sinclair and Volkswagen share a history in South Wales that goes all the way back to 1974. After first becoming established in Port Talbot, Sinclair Volkswagen have successfully expanded and now have a network of Volkswagen sites in Newport, Cardiff, Bridgend, Port Talbot, Swansea and Neyland – Offering Volkswagen new and approved used, finance, servicing, genuine parts and accessories.
